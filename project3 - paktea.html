PROJECT:

Pak Tea House Poster Concept


ROLE:

Graphic Design
Image Making
Typography


INFO:

A promotional poster for Pak Tea House, a real cafe located in Lahore, Pakistan. The poster encapsulates the cafe's historical relevance as a place for thought and reform but caters towards a younger audience. The project is design to be printed and displayed to highlight the atmosphere of Pak Tea House.
